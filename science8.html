Science 8 Curriculum

Explore matter, energy, and living systems through hands-on scientific inquiry.

Course Units

Mix & Flow of Matter

Analyzing fluid dynamics and solution properties

Cells & Systems

Investigating cellular structures and functions

Light & Optical Systems

Exploring light properties and vision science

Mechanical Systems

Understanding simple and complex machines

Water Systems

Studying freshwater and marine ecosystems

Alberta Science 8 Outcomes

  • Conduct controlled experiments using scientific methods
  • Analyze particle theory of matter applications
  • Explain cellular structures and life processes
  • Investigate light properties and optical technologies
  • Calculate mechanical advantage in simple machines
  • Assess human impacts on aquatic ecosystems
  • Communicate scientific findings effectively
  • Apply problem-solving strategies to real-world issues
