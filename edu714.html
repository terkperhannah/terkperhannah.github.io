Course Evaluation Based on UDL Principles

Engagement

Strengths: The self-paced structure promotes autonomy and provides clear outcomes.

Improvements: Add collaborative activities and strategies to address learner variability.

Representation

Strengths: Practice booklets provide consistent instruction.

Improvements: Include videos, visuals, and translated resources for ELL students.

Action and Expression

Strengths: Exams measure understanding effectively.

Improvements: Offer alternative assessments like projects or presentations.

Recommendations for Improvement

  • Engagement: Use gamified activities like Kahoot and schedule weekly group problem-solving sessions.
  • Representation: Create video tutorials and provide glossaries for ELL students.
  • Action and Expression: Add project-based assessments and self-assessment opportunities.
