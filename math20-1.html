Math 20-1

Unit 1: Sequences & Series

Arithmetic & geometric sequences/series; growth & decay; infinite geometric series.

Unit 2: Operations on Radicals

Entire/mixed radicals; add/subtract, multiply/divide radicals; simplification.

Unit 3: Trigonometry

Angles & reference angles, CAST, special triangles & exact values, sine & cosine laws, SSA ambiguity.

Unit 4: Factoring & Applications

Review; trinomials; special forms; solving quadratic & radical equations by factoring.

Unit 5: Quadratic Functions & Equations

Zeros/roots/intercepts; vertex form; completing the square; quadratic formula & discriminant; applications.

Unit 6: Rational Expressions & Equations

Simplify; add/subtract; multiply/divide; solve rational equations & applied problems.

Unit 7: Absolute Value & Reciprocal Functions

Absolute value functions & equations; transformations; reciprocal functions.

Unit 8: Linear & Quadratic Systems and Inequalities

Linear–quadratic & quadratic–quadratic systems; inequalities in one/two variables (with & without tech).

Practice Test / Final Review

Consolidate key concepts across all units with mixed practice and exam prep.
