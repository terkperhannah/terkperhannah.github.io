Alberta Junior High Science Outcomes

The Science curriculum aims to develop students' scientific knowledge, inquiry skills, and appreciation for science in the world around them. Students will:

  • Develop an understanding of key scientific concepts and principles
  • Apply scientific inquiry and problem-solving skills
  • Demonstrate safe and appropriate use of scientific equipment
  • Analyze and interpret data to draw conclusions
  • Explore the relationships between science, technology, and society
  • Communicate scientific ideas effectively

By achieving these outcomes, students will:

  • Develop critical thinking and reasoning skills in scientific contexts
  • Engage in hands-on experiments and investigations
  • Make informed decisions about science-related societal issues
  • Appreciate the role of science in daily life and the natural world
  • Exhibit curiosity and a lifelong interest in scientific exploration
