Math 10C Curriculum

Build foundational mathematical skills for senior high school mathematics.

Course Units

Unit 1: Roots and Powers

Explore exponent laws, radicals, and rational exponents

Unit 2: Trigonometry

Apply right triangle trigonometry to real-world problems

Unit 3: Factors and Products

Master polynomial factoring and algebraic techniques

Unit 4: Relations and Functions

Analyze different function types and their properties

Unit 5: Linear Functions

Investigate slope, equations, and graphical representations

Unit 6: Systems of Equations

Solve linear systems using multiple methods

Unit 7: Measurement

Apply measurement concepts in 2D/3D contexts

Alberta Math 10C Outcomes

  • Apply exponent laws and radical operations
  • Solve right triangle problems using trigonometry
  • Factor polynomials and simplify expressions
  • Analyze function relationships and properties
  • Solve systems of linear equations
  • Calculate measurements for complex shapes
  • Communicate mathematical reasoning effectively
  • Apply problem-solving strategies critically
