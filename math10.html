Alberta Math 10C Outcomes

The Math 10C curriculum builds on prior knowledge and introduces key mathematical concepts that form the foundation for higher-level mathematics. Students will:

  • Develop algebraic skills, including exponent laws, factoring, and solving equations
  • Understand and apply trigonometric ratios to solve right triangle problems
  • Analyze and interpret linear functions and their graphical representations
  • Explore relations and functions, including function notation and properties
  • Solve systems of linear equations using multiple methods
  • Apply measurement concepts to real-world scenarios involving area, surface area, and volume

By achieving these outcomes, students will:

  • Develop confidence in using mathematical reasoning and problem-solving strategies
  • Apply algebraic and geometric concepts in real-world contexts
  • Engage in mathematical communication and critical thinking
  • Recognize the connections between mathematics and other disciplines
  • Exhibit perseverance and curiosity in exploring mathematical challenges
