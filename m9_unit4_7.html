Rotational Symmetry

Answer the following questions about rotational symmetry, angle of rotation, and order of rotation.

1. What is the order of rotational symmetry of an equilateral triangle?

2. What is the angle of rotation for a square with rotational symmetry?

3. What is the order of rotational symmetry for a regular pentagon?

4. What is the smallest angle of rotation for a hexagon with rotational symmetry?
