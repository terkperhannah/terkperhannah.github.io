Math 9 Course Objectives

Program Goals

  • Use mathematics confidently to solve problems
  • Communicate and reason mathematically
  • Appreciate mathematics' value and beauty
  • Connect math to real-world applications
  • Develop lifelong learning skills
  • Become mathematically literate citizens

Student Outcomes

  • Demonstrate positive mathematical mindset
  • Persist through challenging tasks
  • Engage in mathematical discourse
  • Take calculated risks in problem-solving
  • Exhibit curiosity and creativity
  • Apply critical thinking skills
