Welcome to Math 9

Math 9 Course Objective

The main goals of mathematics education are to prepare students to:

  • Use mathematics confidently to solve problems
  • Communicate and reason mathematically
  • Appreciate and value mathematics
  • Make connections between mathematics and its applications
  • Commit to lifelong learning
  • Become mathematically literate adults contributing to society

Students who meet these goals will:

  • Gain an appreciation of mathematics as a science, philosophy, and art
  • Exhibit a positive attitude toward mathematics
  • Engage and persevere in mathematical tasks and projects
  • Contribute to mathematical discussions
  • Take risks in performing mathematical tasks
  • Exhibit curiosity
