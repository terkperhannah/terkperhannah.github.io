Prime Factorization Rational vs. Irrational Integers & Rationals Radicals Mixed Radicals

What You Should Be Able To Do

  • Express numbers as a product of prime factors (including with exponents).
  • Classify numbers within the real number system (natural, whole, integers, rational, irrational).
  • Convert between entire and mixed radicals and simplify radicals.
  • Add, subtract, multiply, and divide radicals when possible.
  • Explain why a number is rational or irrational using definitions and counterexamples.
