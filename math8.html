Welcome to Math 8

Alberta Math 8 Outcomes

The Math 8 curriculum builds on foundational mathematical concepts and introduces more advanced problem-solving strategies. Students will:

  • Develop number sense, including operations with fractions, decimals, and integers
  • Apply algebraic reasoning to solve equations and analyze linear relationships
  • Understand geometric properties, including the Pythagorean theorem and transformations
  • Explore measurement concepts related to prisms, cylinders, and other shapes
  • Analyze and interpret data using probability and statistical methods
  • Apply mathematical reasoning to real-world scenarios and problem-solving situations

By achieving these outcomes, students will:

  • Develop confidence in mathematical thinking and communication
  • Engage in logical reasoning and structured problem-solving
  • Recognize the connections between mathematics and other disciplines
  • Appreciate the practical applications of mathematics in everyday life
  • Demonstrate perseverance and curiosity in mathematical exploration
