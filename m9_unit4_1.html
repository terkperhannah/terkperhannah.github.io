Surface Area Practice Questions

1. Calculate the surface area of a cylinder with a height of 60 cm and a circumference of 62.8 cm. Use 3.14 for π.

2. A rectangular prism has dimensions of 10 cm by 15 cm by 20 cm. Calculate its surface area.

3. Find the surface area of a triangular prism with a base of 8 cm, height of 6 cm, and a side length of 10 cm. The prism is 12 cm long.

4. Calculate the surface area of a cube with a side length of 9 cm.

5. Calculate the surface area of a cylinder with a radius of 7 cm and a height of 14 cm. Use 3.14 for π.

6. A cylinder has a height of 8 cm and a diameter of 10 cm. Use 3.14 for π and calculate its surface area.
