Alberta Math 7 Outcomes

  • Develop number sense with fractions, decimals, and percents
  • Analyze patterns and algebraic relationships
  • Apply geometric properties and transformations
  • Interpret data using statistical methods
  • Solve real-world problems mathematically
  • Communicate mathematical reasoning effectively
  • Demonstrate perseverance in problem-solving
  • Connect mathematics to daily life
