Alberta Math 7 Outcomes

The Math 7 curriculum aims to develop students' mathematical understanding, problem-solving skills, and ability to apply mathematics in real-world situations. Students will:

  • Develop number sense and fluency with fractions, decimals, and percents
  • Recognize and analyze patterns and relationships in mathematical contexts
  • Develop algebraic reasoning and equation-solving skills
  • Understand geometric properties, measurements, and transformations
  • Analyze and interpret data using statistical methods
  • Apply mathematical thinking to problem-solving in various real-world scenarios

By achieving these outcomes, students will:

  • Demonstrate confidence in using mathematical concepts and techniques
  • Engage in mathematical reasoning and critical thinking
  • Develop an appreciation for the role of mathematics in everyday life
  • Communicate mathematical ideas effectively
  • Exhibit perseverance and curiosity in exploring mathematical challenges
